# 🪙 Digital assets (crypto) — rules for YOU

## 🎯 Core principle

👉 Showing crypto balances = **much stronger signal of custody**

Compared to FX, regulators treat this more cautiously.

---

# ✅ When you CAN show digital asset balances

## ✔️ Scenario 1 — External custodian + read-only access

If:

* assets held with licensed custodian
* AMAX has **read-only API access**
* AMAX **cannot withdraw / control assets**

👉 Then you MAY show:

```text
BTC Balance: 0.52
ETH Balance: 3.1
```

BUT you must disclose clearly:

```text
Digital assets are held with external custodians.
AMAX does not custody client assets.
```

---

# ⚠️ Even then — higher risk than FX

Because crypto:

* is bearer-like
* easier to transfer
* often linked to wallet control

👉 Regulators scrutinise this more

---

# ❌ When you should NOT show crypto balances

## 🚫 Your current situation

* No real custodian
* No API access
* Simulation only

👉 Then:

❌ Do NOT show:


Even if simulated — this is risky
