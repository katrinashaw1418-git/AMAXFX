## Important caution on the two new implemented items

These are good additions, but each has limits:

### 1. In-memory idempotency

Good as a short-term safeguard, but not durable across:

* process restarts
* multiple app instances
* horizontal scaling

Safer future form:

```ts
idempotency_keys
- user_id
- route
- key
- response_hash / response_payload
- created_at
- expires_at
```

### 2. In-memory/system-event logging

Helpful for development, but not durable for:

* audits
* incident investigations
* production support

That means it is a valid stopgap, not a final fintech-grade control.
