🧭 Homepage Structure (Recommended)
1. Hero Section (Top of page)

Purpose: Immediately explain what you do

Content:

Headline:

“Secure FX, Digital Wallet & Cryptocurrency Exchange Platform”

Subheading:

“Send, receive, exchange, and manage funds across fiat and digital currencies — built with compliance and security at its core.”

Buttons:
“Get Started” (→ signup/login)
“Learn More”

Optional note (important for pre-launch):

“Platform launching soon, subject to regulatory registration.”

2. Services Overview (3–4 cards)

Purpose: Show your core offerings clearly

Cards:

💱 FX Exchange

Convert between major fiat currencies with competitive rates.

👛 Digital Wallet

Store and manage funds securely in a multi-currency wallet.

₿ Crypto Exchange

Buy, sell, and hold digital assets through a compliant platform.

🌍 Remittance

Send funds domestically and internationally with streamlined processing.

3. How It Works (Simple Flow)

Purpose: Explain your end-to-end model clearly

Example:

Sign up & verify identity (KYC)
Deposit funds (bank transfer / supported methods)
Choose currency (fiat or crypto)
Execute exchange or transfer
Withdraw or hold in wallet

👉 Keep this visual and simple (icons or steps)

4. Compliance & Security Section (VERY important)

Purpose: Build trust with AUSTRAC, banks, and users

Content:

Title:

“Compliance & Risk Management”

Text:

“We are committed to complying with Australian AML/CTF regulations and implementing robust risk management controls.”

Include:
KYC/Identity verification
Transaction monitoring
Suspicious matter reporting
Data security practices

👉 This aligns your business with expectations of AUSTRAC

5. Platform Status / Disclaimer (if pre-launch)

Purpose: Clarify you are not yet live

Example:

“This platform is currently in development and will be launched following completion of regulatory registration and operational readiness.”

6. Target Customers / Use Cases

Purpose: Clarify positioning

Example:

Retail users
SMEs
Cross-border payments
Institutional clients (if applicable)
7. About the Company

Purpose: Establish legitimacy

Include:

Company name
Brief mission statement
Location (Australia-based)
Leadership (optional but strong)
8. Contact Section
Email
Contact form
Business address (your Regus/coworking address)
9. Footer

Include:

Company name
ABN/ACN (if available)
Legal links:
Privacy Policy
Terms & Conditions
AML/CTF statement
Copyright
🔐 Login / Signup Placement
Top navigation:
“Login”
“Sign Up”

👉 But not the main landing page

🎯 Key UX Principle for your model
Public site = informational + trust-building
Login area = transactional platform

👉 This separation is what regulators expect in fintech/crypto businesses

⚠️ What to avoid
Landing page that is just login ❌
Hidden business model ❌
No compliance mention ❌
Overly vague descriptions ❌
💡 Optional advanced sections (if you want stronger credibility)
API / integrations (if institutional focus)
Fees structure (even indicative)
Supported countries/currencies
Risk disclosures
✔️ Summary

Your homepage should:

Clearly explain what you do
Show how it works
Demonstrate compliance awareness
Be accessible without login
Include a clear pre-launch disclaimer (if not live)
