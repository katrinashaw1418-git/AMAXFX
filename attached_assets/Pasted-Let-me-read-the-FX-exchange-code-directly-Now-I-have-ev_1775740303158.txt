### ❌ CRITICAL — One string fails

**Line 423:**
> `By confirming, you authorise AMAX Financial Pty Ltd (ABN 54 690 827 608) to process this foreign exchange transaction.`

**Two problems:**

**Problem 1 — Wrong entity name.** Your ABN 54 690 827 608 is registered to **AMAX Global Pty Ltd**, not "AMAX Financial Pty Ltd." "AMAX Financial" does not exist in your registration. This is a misrepresentation of your legal entity — a direct compliance risk if a regulator or client disputes the transaction.

**Problem 2 — "process this foreign exchange transaction"** is ambiguous. For a non-custodial remittance provider the correct framing is that AMAX *instructs* a regulated banking partner to execute the conversion, not that AMAX processes it directly.

**Fix:**
```
By confirming, you authorise AMAX Global Pty Ltd (ABN 54 690 827 608) 
to instruct our regulated banking partner to execute this foreign 
exchange conversion on your behalf.
```

### ⚠️ CONDITIONAL — One string needs backend verification

This is only true if the FX conversion is purely a ledger rebalance within your partner's system (e.g. Airwallex wallet-to-wallet). Confirm with your banking partner what the actual settlement SLA is and match the UI to that.
