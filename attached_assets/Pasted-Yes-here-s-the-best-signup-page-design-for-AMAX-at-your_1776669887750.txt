## Recommendation

Remove:

* **Continue with Apple**
* **Use Phone Number**

Keep only:

* **Continue with Email**
* **Continue with Google**
* **Sign in**

Because your real model is:

* **Email/Google = authentication**
* **Mobile number = collected in details step**
* **Email OTP = verification**
* **Services = onboarding**

That is cleaner, more premium, and less confusing.
