Target Customers section — Use a 2×2 tile grid rather than a bullet list. Retail Users, SMEs & Businesses, International Transfers, Institutional Clients. Each tile with a short pitch of 1–2 lines. More scannable and looks intentional.

Contact section — Email + a simple contact form (name, email, message). Your registered business address below. For pre-launch, skip phone; email only is fine and expected.
