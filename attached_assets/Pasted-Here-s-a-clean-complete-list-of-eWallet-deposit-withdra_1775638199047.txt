# 💵 FIAT CHANNELS

## ⬇️ Deposits (Add Money)

### ✅ Core Channels (You should DEFINITELY have these)

### 🏦 Bank Transfer (PayID / NPP – Australia)

* Instant bank transfers in Australia
* Uses phone/email/ABN as identifier
* Very low cost
* Real-time confirmation

👉 **Verdict: MUST HAVE (primary rail in AU)**

---

### 💳 Credit / Debit Cards (Visa / Mastercard)

* Fast onboarding
* High conversion
* Works globally

👉 Use: Stripe

👉 **Verdict: MUST HAVE**

---

### 🏦 Open Banking (PayTo / Direct Debit API)

* Direct bank debit via API
* No card fees
* Strong compliance signal

👉 Use:

* Airwallex
* TrueLayer

👉 **Verdict: MUST HAVE (modern fintech standard)**

---

## ⭐ Recommended Channels (Strong UX / growth boost)

### 📱 Apple Pay / Google Pay

* 1-click payments
* Higher mobile conversion

👉 Runs via Stripe

👉 **Verdict: STRONGLY RECOMMENDED**

---

### 🌍 International Bank Transfer (SWIFT)

* Needed for global users
* Slower (1–3 days)

👉 **Verdict: ADD if going international**

---

## ⚠️ Optional / Not Recommended as Core

### 💸 PayPal

* Easy UX but:

  * High fees
  * Limited control
  * Weak for scaling fintech

👉 **Verdict: OPTIONAL (not core infrastructure)**

---

### 🇦🇺 POLi (Legacy AU bank payments)

* Declining usage
* Not future-proof

👉 **Verdict: OPTIONAL / can skip**
