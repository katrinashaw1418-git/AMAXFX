# ⚠️ Compliance Gaps / Red Flags (This is where AUSTRAC will push back)

## 🚨 1. “Contact us for deposit address” (Major Risk)

> “Coinbase Commerce is being configured… contact us”

This is **not acceptable at scale**.

### Why:

* No **automated wallet attribution**
* No **screening before deposit**
* No **audit trail per user wallet**

### AUSTRAC concern:

> “How do you link incoming funds to a verified customer before receipt?”

### Fix:

* Generate **unique deposit address per user**
* Pre-link to KYC profile
* Run **blockchain analytics (Chainalysis/TRM)**

---

## 🚨 2. No Source of Funds / Purpose of Transaction

You collect recipient info — but NOT:

* Purpose of transfer
* Source of funds
* Relationship to beneficiary

### Required under:

* AUSTRAC AML/CTF Act (customer due diligence)
* FATF enhanced due diligence for higher-risk transfers

### Fix:

Add fields like:

* “Purpose of transaction” (dropdown)
* “Source of funds” (salary, trading, investment, etc.)
* “Relationship to beneficiary”

---

## 🚨 3. No Sanctions / PEP Screening Mention

Nothing indicates:

* Sanctions screening
* Politically Exposed Person (PEP) checks

### AUSTRAC expectation:

You must screen:

* Sender
* Recipient
* Wallet address (if possible)

### Fix:

Add:

> “All transactions are screened against global sanctions and AML databases.”

---

## 🚨 4. Travel Rule Data Transmission (Critical Gap)

You collect info — but don’t state:

👉 **HOW you transmit it to the receiving VASP**

This is the **core of the Travel Rule**.

### AUSTRAC will ask:

* Are you using:

  * TRISA?
  * Notabene?
  * Shyft?
  * IVMS101 messaging?

### Fix:

Add something like:

> “Travel Rule data is securely transmitted using IVMS101-compliant protocols to supported VASPs.”

---

## 🚨 5. ETH → Fiat → Bank (Oversimplified)

> “ETH must be converted to fiat first”

This is fine — but incomplete.

### Missing:

* KYC level required for withdrawal
* Bank account ownership verification
* AML monitoring of fiat exit

### Fix:

Add:

* “Bank account must match verified user name”
* “Withdrawals subject to AML/CTF monitoring”

---

## 🚨 6. No Transaction Monitoring Disclosure

You don’t mention:

* Suspicious activity monitoring
* Reporting obligations (SMR/STR)

### Required:

* AUSTRAC expects real-time monitoring

### Fix:

Add:

> “Transactions are monitored for suspicious activity and may be reported to AUSTRAC where required.”

---

## 🚨 7. Internal Transfers = Blind Spot

Internal transfers:

* No blockchain
* No external visibility

👉 Regulators worry about **layering / laundering inside platform**

### Fix:

Add:

* Internal transfer limits
* Monitoring rules
* Audit logs
