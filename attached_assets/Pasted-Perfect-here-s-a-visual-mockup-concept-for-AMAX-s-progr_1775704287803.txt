## **Key Features of This Flow**

1. **Progressive Onboarding:** Minimal upfront friction.
2. **Tickbox KYC Statements:** Explicit consent + signature.
3. **Third-Party Verification:** ID, selfie, optional proof of address.
4. **Conditional Requests:** Only ask additional info if risk flagged.
5. **AUSTRAC-Ready:** Full logging of timestamp, IP, and provider metadata.
6. **Mobile-Optimized:** Especially ID capture and selfies.
