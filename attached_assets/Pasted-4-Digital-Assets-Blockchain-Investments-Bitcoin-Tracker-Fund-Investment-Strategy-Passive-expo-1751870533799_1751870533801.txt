4. Digital Assets & Blockchain Investments
🔷 Bitcoin Tracker Fund
Investment Strategy:
Passive exposure to the price performance of Bitcoin through a regulated, institutionally structured fund. Designed for investors seeking pure Bitcoin price exposure without the operational complexities of direct crypto custody.

Target Return: Variable – tracks Bitcoin price (historical annualized returns have exceeded 100%, but with high volatility)

Structure: Passive tracker fund

Liquidity: Monthly redemptions

Term: Open-ended

Distributions: None (capital appreciation only)

Minimum Investment: $50,000

Risk Profile: High

Return Type: Capital gains

🔷 Early-Stage Token Fund
Investment Strategy:
Participates in pre-launch token investments of blockchain and Web3 projects. Combines venture-style diligence with liquid token opportunities, targeting asymmetric returns from early access and strategic unlocks.

Target Net IRR: 25–40%+

Structure: Hybrid (venture equity + early token allocations)

Liquidity: Illiquid (vesting and lock-up periods)

Term: 4–6 years

Distributions: Event-based (token unlocks/sales)

Minimum Investment: Typically $100,000

Risk Profile: Very High

Return Type: Capital gains + liquidity event-based income

🔷 Pantera Blockchain Fund
Investment Strategy:
Broad-based blockchain exposure via a diversified portfolio including early-stage equity, liquid tokens, and select yield-generating DeFi strategies. Focuses on transformative blockchain infrastructure and financial applications.

Target Net IRR: 20–30%

Structure: Diversified fund (VC + tokens + DeFi)

Liquidity: Long-term lock-in (5–7 years)

Distributions: Periodic based on liquidity events

Minimum Investment: $100,000

Risk Profile: High

Return Type: Blended (capital appreciation, token yield, equity exits)
