Short answer: **No — not directly.**

A customer **cannot withdraw digital assets (crypto/stablecoins) straight to a bank account**. There must be a **conversion step to fiat currency (e.g., AUD)** first.

---

# 💡 How It Actually Works

## 🔁 Correct Flow (What you should implement)

### Step-by-step:

1. **User holds digital asset**

   * e.g. USDT, BTC, ETH

2. **User requests withdrawal**

   * chooses “Withdraw to bank”

3. **System converts crypto → fiat**

   * e.g. USDT → AUD using FX rate

4. **Fiat balance is credited internally**

   * now held as AUD in user account

5. **Bank transfer is executed**

   * via:

     * PayID (Australia)
     * OSKO / NPP
     * or traditional bank rails

---

# ❌ What You CANNOT Do

* Send **crypto directly to a bank account**
* Skip conversion step
* Treat crypto like fiat in banking rails

👉 Banks only accept **fiat currency**

---

# ⚠️ Compliance Angle (Important for AUSTRAC)

If your app is in Australia:

* Crypto → fiat conversion = **regulated activity**
* Bank payout = **remittance activity**

You must:

* KYC the user
* record transaction details
* store FX rate used
