## 1️⃣ Key Implications

### b) Compliance (AUSTRAC / FATF)

* **Travel Rule:** Applies when crypto is moving between wallets that are not self-custody (or between VASPs).
* **AML Monitoring:** You cannot track off-platform balances unless you rely on **client reporting** or an **on-chain verification system**.
* **Audit Trail:** If AMAX doesn’t hold the crypto, **audit logs are weaker**, and regulators may question the enforceability of “internal exchange” balances.

### c) UX & Warnings

* Users **might think AMAX holds their internal wallet balances**.
* Must **clearly communicate** that AMAX is **only facilitating the exchange**, and crypto remains in the user’s control.
* Any “instant transfer” is misleading — it’s really a **trade execution or ledger calculation**.
