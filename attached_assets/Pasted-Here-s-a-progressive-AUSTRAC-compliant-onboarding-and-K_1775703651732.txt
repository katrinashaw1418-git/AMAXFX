Here’s a **progressive, AUSTRAC-compliant onboarding and KYC UI flow for AMAX**, designed to follow **Wise/Revolut best practices** while keeping the experience smooth for the user. I’ve broken it into **steps, screens, and actions**, including the **KYC statements, ID verification, and conditional flows**.

---

## **AMAX Onboarding & KYC Flow – Step by Step**

### **Step 1: Account Creation (Minimal Friction)**

**Screen:** Sign Up
**Fields:**

* Email address
* Password
* Optional: Social login (Google / Apple)

**Action:**

* User clicks **“Create Account”**
* Backend creates a minimal user profile
* Send **email verification link**

**UX Note:** Keep this screen extremely light to reduce drop-off. No KYC statements here.

---

### **Step 2: Email Verification**

**Screen:** Check Your Email
**Action:**

* User clicks verification link
* Status updated to **email verified** in backend

**UX Note:** Cannot proceed to transactions until verified.

---

### **Step 3: Early Risk Profiling (Optional Mini Form)**

**Screen:** How Will You Use Your Account?
**Fields:**

1. Purpose of Account (dropdown) – e.g., Remittance, FX, eWallet, Crypto
2. Products You Plan to Use (checkbox) – eWallet, FX, Crypto, Remittance

**Action:** Store for **risk-based KYC tiering**
**UX Note:** Short form, 2–3 questions. Helps determine if higher KYC is needed later.

---

### **Step 4: Personal Information (Pre-KYC)**

**Screen:** Tell Us About Yourself
**Fields:**

* Full legal name
* Date of birth
* Residential address
* Country of tax residence
* Mobile number (if not already collected)

**Action:** Store in user profile for KYC prefill
**UX Note:** Keep form mobile-friendly, validate inputs.

---

### **Step 5: KYC Statements & Declarations**

**Screen:** Confirm Legal & Compliance Statements
**Format:** Tickboxes + electronic signature
**Statements:**

1. **Identity Declaration:** “All information I provide is true and correct…”
2. **PEP Declaration:** “I am not a PEP nor related to a PEP…”
3. **Sanctions Declaration:** “I am not listed on any sanctions list…”
4. **Source of Funds:** “Funds are derived from legitimate sources…”
5. **Consent to Verification:** “I consent to AMAX/third-party verification…”
6. **Ongoing Monitoring:** “I agree to provide updates if required…”
7. **Accuracy & Compliance:** “I understand providing false information is a criminal offence…”

**Action:**

* User must tick **all boxes**
* User types **full legal name** as electronic signature
* Backend records timestamp and IP for AUSTRAC audit

---

### **Step 6: Identity Verification (Via Veriff/Green ID)**

**Screen:** Verify Your ID
**Action:** Embed or redirect to third-party provider

* Upload government-issued ID (passport, driver’s license, national ID)
* Selfie / liveness check
* Optional: Proof of address (if ID lacks address or flagged by risk model)

**UX Note:** Show progress indicator for each step (ID scan, selfie, verification)

**Backend:** Store verification result, provider metadata, timestamp for AUSTRAC audit

---

### **Step 7: Conditional Additional KYC**

**Trigger:** Only if:

* Transaction is high-risk
* Large deposits / withdrawals
* Account flagged by risk engine

**Fields:**

* Source of Wealth
* Employment / Income Information
* Additional Proof of Address (if not verified previously)

**UX Note:** Only request when necessary to **minimize user friction**

---

### **Step 8: Verification Review & Unlock**

**Screen:** Account Verified
**Action:**

* Update user status: Verified / Pending / Rejected
* Unlock transactions only for Verified users
* Notify user in-app and via email

**UX Note:** Show clear instructions if verification is rejected, and allow **resubmission**

---

### **Step 9: Full Access & Ongoing Monitoring**

**Screen:** Dashboard / Full Access
**Action:**

* User can access eWallet, FX, Crypto, Remittance
* Continuous monitoring for AML/CTF compliance
* Re-verification triggered by:

  * Changes to personal info
  * PEP / sanctions updates
  * High-risk transactions

**Backend:** Maintain records for **7 years** per AML/CTF Act

---

## **Key UX Principles**

1. **Progressive Disclosure:** Only ask what’s necessary at each step.
2. **Explicit Consent:** Tickboxes + electronic signature before KYC.
3. **Conditional Fields:** Source of funds / proof of address only when required.
4. **Audit-Ready:** Each step logged with timestamp, IP, and provider metadata.
5. **Mobile-Friendly:** All steps designed for seamless mobile capture (ID + selfie).
6. **Error Handling:** Clear feedback if verification fails, allow re-upload.
