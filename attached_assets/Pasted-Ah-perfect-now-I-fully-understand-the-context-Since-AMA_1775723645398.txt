Ah — perfect, now I fully understand the context. Since **AMAX is not a custodian**, the current “internal ledger” approach doesn’t logically work. We need to **redesign the exchange page as a front-end to an external custodian (Coinbase) while simplifying compliance flows**. Here’s the reasoning and proposed redesign:

---

## **Key Principles for the Redesign**

1. **AMAX is non-custodial**

   * Crypto is **never held on AMAX accounts**.
   * All purchases / sales happen via **Coinbase (external custodian)**.

2. **Compliance is centralized**

   * **KYC, AML, and Travel Rule** are already handled on the **Compliance/KYC page**.
   * Exchange page does **not collect source of funds**; it only facilitates execution.

3. **Ledger-based “internal balance” no longer applies**

   * User balance on AMAX is **just a reflection of completed trades**, not actual custody.
   * Exchange page becomes a **trade execution interface** to Coinbase.

4. **Simplify UX**

   * Remove unnecessary fields like “Source of Funds”.
   * Only display **fiat → crypto** or **crypto → fiat** amounts.
   * Include clear **non-custodial notice**.

---

## **Proposed Redesign — Coinbase-Integrated Exchange Page**

### **Header**

**Title:** `Digital Asset Exchange (via Coinbase)`
**Info Box / Badge:**

> 🔒 **Non-Custodial Trade Execution**
> All trades are executed through **Coinbase as the custodian**. AMAX does not hold your crypto or private keys. Transactions are irreversible once executed.

---

### **Step 1: Select Trade**

**Buy Crypto (Fiat → Crypto)**

* From: Fiat → `AUD / USD`
* Input: Amount to spend

**Sell Crypto (Crypto → Fiat)**

* From: Crypto → `BTC / ETH / USDT / USDC`
* Input: Amount to sell

**To: Asset Class**

* Dropdown: `BTC / ETH / USDT / USDC` or fiat `AUD / USD`

**Swap button** → toggle buy/sell

---

### **Step 2: Trade Summary (Dynamic)**

| Label               | Value                                                |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **You Send**        | e.g., 500 AUD                                        |
| **Spot Rate**       | e.g., 1 AUD = 0.00000994 BTC                         |
| **Processing Fee**  | e.g., 0.5%                                           |
| **You Receive**     | Calculated crypto/fiat                               |
| **Settlement Type** | Coinbase custodial execution (non-custodial on AMAX) |

**Tooltip:**

> “Crypto is sent directly to your Coinbase wallet. AMAX does not hold crypto or private keys. Transactions are irreversible. Digital assets are subject to market volatility.”

---

### **Step 3: Review & Confirm**

* **Checkbox:**

> “I confirm this trade and acknowledge AMAX does not custody crypto. Trade execution is handled via Coinbase.”

* **Button:** `Review & Confirm Exchange` → opens confirmation modal:

  * Re-display trade amounts, fees, spot rate
  * Warning about **irreversibility**
  * Confirm execution via Coinbase API

---

### **Step 4: Execution**

* On confirm, **AMAX sends trade instructions to Coinbase**
* User receives crypto/funds in **their Coinbase account**
* AMAX page updates **display only**, reflecting the executed trade

---

### **Step 5: Post-Trade Info**

* Confirmation Banner:

> “Your trade has been executed via Coinbase. Your crypto is in your Coinbase wallet. AMAX does not hold private keys.”

* Links / Optional Buttons:

  * `View in Coinbase`
  * `Return to Exchange`

---

### **UX / Compliance Advantages**

1. **No misleading internal balances** — crypto resides in **Coinbase wallets**.
2. **Simplified form** — no source of funds, no external wallet input.
3. **Compliance already centralized** — KYC/AML handled on KYC page.
4. **Clear non-custodial messaging** — prevents regulatory confusion.
5. **Scalable** — adding more crypto/fiat pairs is simple.

---

💡 **Bottom Line:**

* AMAX acts as a **trade execution portal** only.
* Coinbase handles custody.
* KYC/AML is centralized elsewhere.
* UX is simple: select asset, amount, review, confirm.
* Ledger-based “internal balance” is removed — only **display post-trade reflection**.
